**Research from Quantpedia:**

## 4. Funding Rate Arbitrage (Delta Neutral Income)

### The Strategy

**How It Works:**
1. Buy 1 BTC in spot market (delta = +1)
2. Short 1 BTC in perpetual futures (delta = -1)
3. Net delta = 0 (price-neutral)
4. Collect funding payments every 8 hours

**When Funding Rate is Positive:**
- Longs pay shorts
- You receive funding for your short position
- Typical rate: 0.01-0.05% per 8 hours

**Annualized Returns:**

- 2024 data: BTC funding averaged +0.47% (64% annualized)

| Asset Type | Maximum Allocation |

## 7. Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) for Crypto-Only Portfolios

### Enhanced DCA Strategy

**Basic DCA:**
- Invest fixed amount monthly regardless of price
- Reduces timing risk
- Smoothes entry points

**Enhanced DCA for Crypto:**

**Rule 1: Base Monthly Investment**
- Invest 60% of monthly allocation regardless of conditions

**Rule 2: Trend Adjustment**
- When price > 200-day MA: Reduce to 40% (trend following shows extended)
- When price < 200-day MA: Increase to 80% (accumulation phase)

**Rule 3: Volatility Scaling**
- High volatility (>100% annualized): Reduce position size by 25%
- Low volatility (<50% annualized): Increase position size by 25%

**Rule 4: Allocation Within Crypto**
- 50% BTC (always the foundation)
- 30% ETH
- 20% split among 3-5 altcoins or stablecoins

### Bybit-Specific DCA Implementation

**Recurring Buy Feature:**
- Set up automatic purchases
- Choose daily, weekly, or monthly intervals
- Split between BTC and ETH

**Manual Enhanced DCA:**
- Check 200-day MA before each purchase
- If above MA: Buy 50% of normal amount, save 50% stablecoins
- If below MA: Buy 150% of normal amount (from stablecoin reserves)

### DCA Research Findings

**Grayscale Research:**
- DCA reduces maximum drawdown vs lump sum
- DCA underperforms lump sum in strong uptrends
- Best for: Investors without large existing positions
- Best for: Risk-averse investors in volatile assets
